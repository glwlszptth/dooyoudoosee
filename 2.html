I’m a set designer and prop stylist,
working in London. My study began in fine arts.
I worked in film and advertising for several years
before moving into my chosen field.For someone who
loves art and objects — from the most magnificent
to the seemingly mundane — it was an effortless
evolution.

Connections. I look for them everywhere. In architecture,
furniture, design, food, fabric, fauna, geometry,
photography and travel. I find elements that work together
— or clash magnificently — and fuse them into a wholly
original vision. I’m here to help you turn your brief
into something brilliant.
